How did South Africa become the most unequal place in the world?

Tanzania has the closest population size to South Africa's and has been reported on in the UNDP report as a country that had an inverted U-shaped income inequality trend. The political climate in Tanzania is very interesting to investigate because of the nature of its culture. As multiple nations came together to form the United Republic of Tanzania, they have a record of opening borders and democratically progressing towards a more representative population in the government.
